Zoologist



I’m studying Zoology at the University of Aberdeen—close to home and the perfect fit for my love of animals and marine life. Alongside core science modules, I’ve chosen language, computing, and accounting to support my interests in travelling, investing, and programming. These subjects also tie into my passion for game design and investing, giving me a well-rounded mix of science, creativity, and practical skills.

Animals



Growing up, I spent lots of time outdoors and visited zoos and museums whenever I could, eventually starting a fossil collection. My love for animals was shaped by a home full of pets, a natural empathy for creatures, and a fascination sparked by books and movies such as jurassic park.

SCUBA DIVING

In my second year, I took the plunge—literally—and discovered a whole new way to connect with the water I’ve always loved. Growing up near the coast, I’ve always felt at home in the ocean. As a kid, I spent hours bodyboarding, and now, diving has opened up a completely different world beneath the surface. It’s sparked a deeper appreciation for marine life and the environment, and I’ve met some of the most supportive, adventurous people through the club. Next on my list? Surfing.
